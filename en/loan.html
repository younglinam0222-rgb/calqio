Loan Calculator — Complete Guide

Understanding the true cost of a loan upfront is essential for financial planning. Over 30 years, total interest can exceed the original principal.

Amortization Formula

Monthly Payment = P × [r(1+r)ⁿ] ÷ [(1+r)ⁿ - 1] P: Loan Amount | r: Monthly Rate (annual ÷ 12) | n: Total Months

Example: $300,000 at 4.5% for 30 years → $1,520/month, total interest ~$247,000.

💡 Even 0.5% lower rate can save tens of thousands in total interest. Always shop around.
