Target Price Calculator — Complete Guide

Setting your target and stop-loss before buying eliminates emotional decision-making. Disciplined traders always define their exit before entry.

Formulas

Target Price = Buy × (1 + Target% ÷ 100) Stop-Loss = Buy × (1 - Stop% ÷ 100) Risk/Reward = Target% ÷ Stop%

Example: Buy $50, Target +20%, Stop -10% → Target $60, Stop $45, R/R 2.0.

Why Risk/Reward Matters

With 2:1 R/R, you profit overall even with only a 50% win rate. Professional traders require minimum 2:1.

💡 Execute your stop-loss mechanically. Loss aversion bias leads to far bigger losses.
