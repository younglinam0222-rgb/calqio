Capital Gains Tax Calculator — Complete Guide

Understanding capital gains tax is crucial for calculating your true investment returns. Tax rates and rules vary significantly by country and holding period.

Formula

Capital Gain = Sale - Purchase - Fees Taxable Amount = Gain - Deduction Tax Due = Taxable Amount × Rate

Tax Loss Harvesting

Sell losing positions before year-end to offset gains and reduce your taxable income. This strategy, done annually, can save thousands in taxes.

💡 Consult a tax advisor for your specific situation — tax laws change frequently.
