Return Rate Calculator — Complete Guide

Your real return must account for trading fees. Brokers typically charge 0.015%–0.25% per trade, which significantly impacts short-term trading profits.

Return Rate Formula

Return (%) = (Sell Total - Buy Total - Fees) ÷ Buy Total × 100

Example: Buy 100 shares at $50, sell at $65. With 0.1% fee each way, actual return is ~29.82%, not 30%.

Impact of Fees on Returns

Trading $10,000 ten times per month at 0.1% round-trip costs ~$12,000/year in fees — equivalent to losing 12% annually. Reducing unnecessary trades is crucial.

💡 Enter 0 for fees if you don't know your broker's rate. Results will exclude fees.
